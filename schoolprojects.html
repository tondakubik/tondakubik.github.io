Computer solutions of physical problems - NMMO212

  • Solving physics problems with mathematical and programming tools (Mathematica, Matlab, etc.). Improving command line and GitHub skills, and teamwork
  • mathematical pendulum, firing control, systems of coupled oscillators, airship navigation, chaotic waterwheel, inverted pendulum, Paul trap, Chladni figures and more...
  • some of the results:
    • How to fly an airship to point (0,0) when there is an unpleasant headwind.
    • How to fly an airship from Prague to Ostrava as quickly as possible in normal weather conditions
    • How to fly an airship from Prague to Ostrava as quickly as possible in not-so-normal weather conditions (with storms and a tornado devastating Moravia) -> 14.9 hours
    • How to fly an airship from Prague to Ostrava in not-so-normal weather conditions (with storms and a tornado devastating Moravia) without violating Polish airspace -> 18.9 hours
    • How to aim my cannon at an enemy cleverly hiding behind a hill.
    • Spectral collocation method and eigenvalue problem for laplace operator – Chladni figures: rectangular domain
